M2tof

Syntax

TOF = M2tof(M0,M,n)
TOF = M2tof(M0,M,n,k)

Description

TOF = M2tof(M0,M,n) finds the time of flight between the initial mean anomaly, , and the current mean anomaly, , given the mean motion, .

TOF = M2tof(M0,M,n,k) finds the time of flight between the initial mean anomaly, , and the current mean anomaly, , given the mean motion, , and the integer number of completed orbits, .

Input/Output Parameters

Input M0 initial mean anomaly 1×1
double		 rad

k (OPTIONAL) integer number of completed orbits (defaults to 0) 1×1

double		 s

Example #1: Time of flight within a single orbit.

Find the time of flight (in hours) from an initial mean anomaly (at time ) to the current mean anomaly (at time ) for an orbit with mean motion .

Finding the time of flight in seconds,

TOF = M2tof(pi/2,3*pi/2,0.0011);

Converting the time of flight to hours,

TOF = TOF/3600

TOF =

    0.7933

Example #2: Time of flight for multiple orbits.

Find the time of flight (in hours) corresponding from an initial mean anomaly (at time ) to the current mean anomaly (at time ) for an orbit with mean motion . Note that orbits have been completed between time and time .

Finding the time of flight in seconds,

TOF = M2tof(pi/2,3*pi/2,0.0011,5);

Converting the time of flight to hours,

TOF = TOF/3600

TOF =

    8.7266
