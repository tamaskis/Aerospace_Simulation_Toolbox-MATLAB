n2a

Semi-major axis from mean motion.

Syntax

a = n2a(n)
a = n2a(n,mu)

Description

a = n2a(n) finds the semi-major axis, , given the mean motion, , for a circular () or elliptical () orbit around the Earth.

a = n2a(n,mu) finds the semi-major axis, , given the mean motion, , for a circular () or elliptical () orbit around a cental body with gravitational parameter .

Input/Output Parameters

Variable Symbol Description Format Units
Input n mean motion 1×1
double		 rad/s
mu (OPTIONAL) gravitational parameter 1×1
double		 m3/s2
Output a semi-major axis 1×1
double		 m

Example #1: Orbit around the Earth.

Find the semi-major axis for an orbit around the Earth with mean motion .

Finding the semi-major axis in meters,

a = n2a(0.0011);

Converting the semi-major axis to kilometers,

a = 0.001*a

a =

   6.9064e+03

Example #2: Orbit around the moon.

Find the semi-major axis for an orbit around the Moon with mean motion .

Gravitational parameter of the moon (in ),

mu = MU_MOON;

Finding the semi-major axis in meters,

a = n2a(4e-4,mu);

Converting the semi-major axis to kilometers,

a = 0.001*a

a =

   3.1293e+03
