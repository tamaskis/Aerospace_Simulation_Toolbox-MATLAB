Rotation Matrices

  • matchain Chaining passive rotations represented by rotation matrices.
  • matrotate Passive rotation of a vector by a rotation matrix.
  • rot1 Rotation matrix for a passive rotation about the 1st axis.
  • rot2 Rotation matrix for a passive rotation about the 2nd axis.
  • rot3 Rotation matrix for a passive rotation about the 3rd axis.
  • rot313 Rotation matrix for the 3-1-3 rotation sequence.
  • rot321 Rotation matrix for the 3-2-1 rotation sequence.

Quaternions

  • quatchain Chaining passive rotations represented by unit quaternions.
  • quatconj Conjugate of a quaternion.
  • quatinv Inverse of a quaternion.
  • quatmul Quaternion multiplication (Hamilton product).
  • quatnorm Norm of a quaternion.
  • quatnormalize Normalize a quaternion.
  • quatrotate Passive rotation of a vector by a unit quaternion.

Conversions Between Rotation Parameterizations

  • axang2eul_321 Axis-angle representation to 3-2-1 Euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll).
  • axang2mat Axis-angle representation to rotation matrix.
  • axang2quat Axis-angle representation to unit quaternion.
  • eul2axang_321 3-2-1 Euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll) to axis-angle representation.
  • eul2mat_321 3-2-1 Euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll) to rotation matrix.
  • eul2quat_321 3-2-1 Euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll) to unit quaternion.
  • mat2axang Rotation matrix to axis-angle representation.
  • mat2eul_321 Rotation matrix to 3-2-1 Euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll).
  • mat2quat Rotation matrix to unit quaternion.
  • quat2axang Quaternion to axis-angle representation.
  • quat2eul_321 Quaternion to 3-2-1 Euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll).
  • quat2mat Quaternion to rotation matrix.
